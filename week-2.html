Week Two — 3–7 August

Overview Week 1 Week 2 Week 3

A week of circus, story and outdoor adventure.

Our second week brings a little more sparkle — two days of circus skills, a day of theatre, and all the outdoor adventure in between. Set on the edge of the New Forest, with nature all around us, every day has its own theme and plenty of room to play.

Screen-free and led by our Waldorf-trained team, it's a week of trying new things, finding your courage and having a wonderful time.

The week, day by day
Mon3 AUGUST

Circus day 1

Roll up! A full day under the big top — making circus toys to keep, practising juggling, balancing and circus skills, and finishing with a show. A complete circus day in itself.

Tue4 AUGUST

Circus day 2

A full day of circus magic — making circus toys, learning new skills and tricks, and bringing it together for a joyful circus show. Everything packed into one day.

Wed5 AUGUST

Theatre day

A day of drama games and storytelling, building our own theatre and stepping up for a show by day's end — full of imagination and courage.

Thu6 AUGUST

Explorer day

With maps in hand the children explore on nature walks and treasure hunts — making and solving cryptic clues, beating the clock and overcoming obstacles — then turn their hands to some raw food creations to make and taste together.

Fri7 AUGUST

Water fun day

We round off the week outdoors with water slides, model boats, bubbles and obstacle courses in the summer sun.

Nurturing bright young futures with confidence, creativity and compassion.
